About me

I am a highly motivated person who has some experiences in developing some basic websites applications as a front-end programmer, version management with Git.

Currently I just graduated from Del Institute of Technology, majoring in Information Systems and am looking for a job that fits my major.

In August 2019, I completed my internship as a frontend Web developer. As a frontend developer for a website that I am developing, I use a framework, namely vuejs. During my internship, apart from being a frontend developer, I was also challenged to become a system analyst. As a systems analyst I was challenged to use analysis and design techniques to solve business problems using information technology. A Systems analysts may serve as change agents who identify the organizational improvements needed, design systems to implement those changes, and train and motivate others to use the systems

First, being given the challenge of being a systems analyst feels great because it is very relevant to what I learned at campus, namely the system requirements analysis course. There are many interesting challenges in every system analysis and design process, namely how to document and analyze every user need to turn it into an attractive design.

I have always been interested in learning the basics of software development, starting from requirements gathering, defining user requirements, analyzing them, designing them, determining the right technology for development (for example the right programming language for development or using a more effective framework). I also feel comfortable working in a team, and also independently.
